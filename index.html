Clearra Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Clearra handles information when you use the Clearra gallery cleaner and media organization app.

1. About Clearra

Clearra is a gallery cleaner and media organization app. It helps users scan, organize, preview, compress, and clean photos and videos on their device.

Clearra is developed by Self Meta Labs. You can contact the developer at ergfurkancangi@gmail.com.

2. Photos and Videos

  • Photos and videos are processed on the user's device.
  • Clearra does not upload photos or videos to a server.
  • The app may request access to photos and videos only to scan, organize, preview, compress, or help the user clean media.
  • Media content, thumbnails, file paths, file names, and hashes are not sent to analytics.

3. Account Information

No account, email, or password is required for basic use of Clearra.

4. User Control and Deletion

  • The user always decides what to delete.
  • Clearra does not automatically delete media without user confirmation.
  • When media is deleted, the action follows the behavior provided by the user's device, operating system, or gallery app.

5. Analytics and Crash Reporting

If analytics or crash reporting is enabled, Clearra may collect only technical diagnostic data such as device type, app version, crash logs, and usage events. This information is used to improve reliability and app performance.

Clearra does not send media content, thumbnails, file paths, file names, or hashes to analytics.

6. Purchases and Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Google Play Billing. Google Play may process payment and subscription-related data according to Google's own policies.

Users can manage subscriptions through Google Play.

7. Rewarded Ads

Rewarded ads may be added later. Ads do not require uploading user photos or videos.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Clearra's privacy practices, contact Self Meta Labs at ergfurkancangi@gmail.com.
